A Story Culture ∞

From a disparate set of information, you continually find your own arc, your own story, and my question is: What are you going to do with it?
You’ve got two things going for you. You’ve got the inexplicable moment of inspiration that created your idea, and it’s the closest thing to magic you’ll experience in your life.
In this digitally distant world full of information that appears to only be moving faster and faster, you get to choose: how much will I consume and how much will I create?
